F1 ELO Rankings

A custom Elo rating system for every Formula 1 driver, updated after every Grand Prix. Rather than copying existing head-to-head teammate models, I built my own weighted performance scoring pipeline from scratch — iteratively recalculating driver ratings using race performance scores, a delta against their pre-race Elo, and a fixed weighting factor.

To make abstract numbers meaningful, ratings are mapped onto a linear interpolation against F1 25 game ratings — so you can actually visualise what a score of 1721 means in practice.

JavaScript HTML/CSS Data Modelling Elo Algorithm Linear Interpolation
Example: Pierre Gasly (Bahrain)
Race rating 8.6
Perf. score 1000 + (8.6 × 100) = 1860
Elo delta 1860 − 1714 = 146
× K factor 146 × 0.05 = +7
New Elo 1721
